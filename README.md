### Activity recap (as briefed)
- Platform: French Atelier organic social (Meta / Instagram).
- Mechanic: followers/ambassadors **share our channel link** with their network.
- Qualify: send a **print-screen of ≥10 shares** (channel-link messages sent) + fill the short form. **Min 10 shares to qualify.**
- Win: **top 3 sharers** win a round-trip flight USA → Paris.
- Selection: committee (shared activity, new followers linked to shares, engagement level).
- Flights: US departure Jul 10/11/12 → return Jul 13/14/15. Changes: July only, +$80/ticket.
- Notification: phone + text + email; no response within 7 days → ticket goes to alternate.
