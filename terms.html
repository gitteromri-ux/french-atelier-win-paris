Effective June 2026. These Official Rules govern the “Win a Free Flight to Paris for Bastille Day” contest (the “Promotion”), run by French Atelier (operated by Acadomia). No purchase necessary to enter or win. By entering, you agree to these rules and to the Sponsor’s decisions, which are final.

01Eligibility

Open to individuals who are 18 or older (or the age of majority where they live) at the time of entry, who are legally able to travel to France on the eligible dates and hold valid travel documents. Employees of the Sponsor and Acadomia, their affiliates, and their immediate families and households are not eligible. Void where prohibited.

02How to Enter

  • Follow French Atelier on Instagram and Meta — @livefrenchatelier.
  • Share the official French Atelier channel with at least ten (10) friends.
  • Upload a screenshot showing your shares with the official entry form.
A minimum of ten (10) shares is required to qualify. Limit one entry per person; duplicate, automated or fraudulent entries are disqualified.

03Winner Selection

This is a skill- and merit-based contest, not a game of chance. A French Atelier committee selects our biggest and most genuine sharers — judged on the reach, consistency and authenticity of their shares. A valid screenshot is required for any entry to be considered, and the committee’s decisions are final.

04The Prize

  • A flight to Paris, which French Atelier arranges and coordinates for the winner, timed around the Bastille Day celebrations of July 2026.
  • A place on the guided French Atelier Bastille Day program as published on the Promotion page.
  • The prize covers the flight and the guided program only; other personal expenses are the winner’s responsibility. The prize is non-transferable, has no cash alternative, and may be substituted with one of equal or greater value if needed.

05Winner Notification

Winners are contacted using the phone number and email address on their entry form, including by their official prize email from French Atelier. A winner who does not respond within seven (7) days of the first attempt forfeits the prize, and an alternate may be selected. Once details are confirmed, French Atelier arranges and coordinates the flight booking with the winner.

06General Conditions

  • The Sponsor may disqualify any entrant who tampers with the entry process or uses fake, purchased or automated shares, and may modify, suspend or end the Promotion if its integrity is compromised.
  • By accepting a prize, winners allow the Sponsor to use their name, handle and entry materials for promotion, except where prohibited by law. Information submitted is used only to administer the Promotion and is never sold.
  • To the extent permitted by law, the Sponsor is released from liability arising from participation or prize use; the prize is accepted “as is.”
  • This Promotion is not sponsored, endorsed or administered by Meta, Facebook or Instagram.
