Win a Flight to Paris

Terms & Conditions

French Atelier by ACADOMIA · Last updated June 2026

These Official Rules govern the French Atelier “Win a Flight to Paris” contest (the “Contest”). By entering, you agree to these Rules and to the decisions of the Sponsor, which are final and binding on all matters relating to the Contest. No purchase is necessary to enter or win. A purchase will not improve your chances of winning.

1 The Sponsor

The Contest is run by French Atelier by ACADOMIA (the “Sponsor,” “we,” “us”). The Contest is in no way sponsored, endorsed, administered by, or associated with Instagram, Meta, or any other social platform. You understand that you are providing your information to the Sponsor and not to any social platform.

2 Who Can Enter

  • You must be 18 years of age or older at the time of entry.
  • You must hold a valid passport (or be able to obtain one in time for travel) and be legally able to travel internationally to France.
  • Employees of the Sponsor and their immediate family or household members are not eligible.
  • The Contest is void where prohibited or restricted by law.

Entrants are responsible for ensuring they meet all eligibility requirements, including any visa, passport, or travel-document requirements that apply to them.

3 How to Enter

To enter the Contest, you must:

  • Follow our official account, @livefrenchatelier.
  • Share our channel with at least ten (10) friends.
  • Submit proof of your shares, along with your name, phone number, and social handle, through the entry form on our Contest page.

Two ways to submit your proof — both count equally:

  • One group share. Share our channel to a group of ten or more friends at once, then upload a single screenshot that clearly shows it was sent to all of them.
  • Separate shares. Share our channel with friends one at a time, then upload a screenshot of each individual share.

Whichever method you choose, your entry must clearly demonstrate that you have shared our channel with at least ten friends. Entries that do not clearly show the required shares may be disqualified at the Sponsor's discretion. Incomplete, illegible, or fraudulent entries are void.

4 How Winners Are Chosen

Winners will be selected by the French Atelier committee from among valid entrants who have shared our channel with the most friends, subject to the minimum requirement above. The committee's selection is final. The number of winners is at the sole discretion of the Sponsor and may vary.

The Sponsor reserves the right to verify any entry and to request additional proof of sharing before confirming a winner. Failure to provide satisfactory verification may result in disqualification.

5 Winner Notification

Important

We will contact valid winners directly — by phone and from our official email address — in early June 2026 or before. We will never ask a winner to pay a fee, send money, or share a password or payment details in order to claim a prize.

If a selected winner cannot be reached, does not respond within a reasonable time after we attempt contact, declines the prize, or is found to be ineligible or in breach of these Rules, the Sponsor may disqualify that entrant and select an alternate winner.

6 The Prize

The prize is a round-trip flight from the United States to Paris, France, together with participation in the French Atelier guided program timed around Bastille Day in July 2026.

Flights are flexible

We do not depart from any single fixed airport. After a winner is confirmed, our representative will be in contact to arrange the flight from an airport near the winner, subject to availability and reasonable routing. Specific dates, times, airline, and routing are determined by the Sponsor.

What the prize includes

  • A round-trip flight to Paris, booked by the Sponsor from an airport near the winner.
  • Participation in the French Atelier guided program during the program dates.
Please read carefully

What the prize does not include

The prize is not an all-expenses-paid trip. Unless expressly stated above, the prize does not include and the winner is solely responsible for:

  • Hotel and any accommodation
  • Meals, food, and drinks
  • Local transport, transfers to and from airports, and travel within Paris
  • Travel insurance, passport and visa fees, and any travel documents
  • Spending money and any personal expenses
  • Any taxes, baggage fees, or other costs not expressly included above

The prize is non-transferable and may not be exchanged or redeemed for cash, except that the Sponsor may, at its sole discretion, substitute a prize of equal or greater value if the stated prize becomes unavailable for any reason. The winner is responsible for any taxes that may apply to the prize. Travel is subject to the terms and conditions of the airline and any third-party providers, and to applicable travel advisories and regulations.

7 Your Information & Privacy

When you enter, you provide your name, phone number, social handle, and screenshots of your shares. We use this information only to administer the Contest — to verify entries, select and contact winners, and arrange a prize. We do not sell your information, and we do not share it with third parties except as needed to operate the Contest (for example, to arrange flights) or as required by law.

Uploaded screenshots are used solely to confirm eligibility and are not published or used for any other purpose without your consent. You may contact us to request that we delete your entry information after the Contest concludes.

8 Fair Play

Entries that are fraudulent, automated, manipulated, or that misrepresent the entrant's sharing activity will be disqualified. The Sponsor reserves the right to disqualify any entrant who tampers with the entry process, violates these Rules, or acts in a disruptive or unsporting manner.

These Rules are the complete and exclusive agreement regarding the Contest. If any provision is found unenforceable, the remaining provisions remain in full effect. These Rules are governed by applicable law in the Sponsor's jurisdiction.

10 Questions

For any questions about the Contest or these Rules, reach out to us through our official account, @livefrenchatelier, or our official email. Always confirm you are speaking with an official French Atelier representative before sharing any personal details.
