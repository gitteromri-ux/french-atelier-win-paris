01The Promotion

"Win a Free Flight to Paris for Bastille Day" (the "Contest") is a social-media promotion run by French Atelier (by Acadomia) across our Meta and Instagram channels. The Contest invites our followers and brand ambassadors to share the French Atelier channel with their network for the chance to win one of three round-trip flights to Paris, timed for the Bastille Day (Fête Nationale) celebrations of July 2026.

02Eligibility

  • Open to followers and brand ambassadors of French Atelier who complete all entry requirements below.
  • Participants must be able to depart from a major airport in the United States (see the eligible US airports list below).
  • Participants must hold valid travel documentation (e.g. a passport valid for the travel dates) and be legally able to travel to France.
  • Employees of French Atelier / Acadomia and their immediate families are not eligible.

03How to Enter

To enter and qualify for the win, a participant must complete all of the following:

  • Follow French Atelier on Instagram and Meta.
  • Share our channel link with friends, family and your network. A participant must make a minimum of 10 shares (channel-link messages sent) to qualify.
  • Capture a print-screen showing at least 10 shares — the channel-link messages you sent — as proof of entry.
  • Complete the short entry form and submit it together with your screenshot.
A minimum of 10 shares is required to qualify for the win. Entries showing fewer than 10 channel-link share messages will not be eligible.

04Selection of Winners

The top three participants by sharing will each win a flight. Winners are selected by a French Atelier committee, which examines:

  • Shared activity on social media — the breadth and consistency of channel-link shares.
  • New channel followers linked to the participant's shares.
  • Engagement level and authenticity of the reach generated.

The committee's decisions are final. A valid screenshot demonstrating the 10-share minimum is required for any entry to be considered.

05The Prize

  • Three (3) winners each receive one (1) round-trip flight between the United States and Paris, timed for the Bastille Day celebrations.
  • Winners also receive access to the French Atelier three-day insider program of recommendations (terrasses, hidden gems, museums, cruises and curated dining) as published on the Contest landing page.
  • The prize is the flight as described; it is non-transferable except as expressly provided in these terms and has no cash alternative.

07Winner Notification

  • Winners will be contacted by phone call, text message and email.
  • A winner who has been contacted and fails to respond within a 7-day period will no longer be eligible, and that ticket will be transferred to an alternative winner.
  • Winners may be asked to confirm eligibility and provide travel details before tickets are issued.
