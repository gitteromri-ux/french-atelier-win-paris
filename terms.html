Effective date: June 2026 · Last updated: June 2026. These Official Rules govern the “Win a Free Flight to Paris for Bastille Day” contest (the “Promotion”). Please read them carefully and retain a copy for your records.

01Sponsor & Promotion

The “Win a Free Flight to Paris for Bastille Day” promotion (the “Promotion”) is sponsored and administered by French Atelier, operated by Acadomia (the “Sponsor,” “we,” “us” or “our”). The Promotion is a skill- and merit-based contest conducted through the Sponsor’s official Meta (Facebook) and Instagram channels, inviting eligible participants to share the French Atelier channel with their network for the chance to win one of three (3) round-trip flights to Paris, timed for the Bastille Day (Fête Nationale) celebrations of July 2026.

These Official Rules form a binding agreement between each participant and the Sponsor. By entering, you accept and agree to be bound by these Official Rules and by the decisions of the Sponsor, which are final and binding in all matters relating to the Promotion.

02Promotion Period

The Promotion begins at the date and time announced on the Sponsor’s official channels and the Promotion landing page (the “Start Date”) and closes at the entry deadline stated on those channels (together, the “Promotion Period”). Entries submitted before the Start Date or after the entry deadline will not be eligible. The Sponsor’s system clock is the official timekeeping device for the Promotion.

No purchase or payment of any kind is necessary to enter or win. A purchase will not improve a participant’s chances of winning.

03Eligibility

  • The Promotion is open only to individuals who are at least eighteen (18) years of age (or the age of majority in their jurisdiction of residence, whichever is greater) at the time of entry.
  • Participants must be able to depart from one of the eligible major US airports listed in Section 8, and must be legally able to enter and travel to France on the eligible travel dates.
  • Participants must hold, and are solely responsible for obtaining, valid travel documentation — including a passport valid for the duration of travel and any required visa or entry authorization.
  • Employees, officers, directors, agents and representatives of the Sponsor, Acadomia and their respective affiliates, and the immediate family (spouse, parent, child, sibling) and household members of each, are not eligible.
  • The Promotion is void where prohibited or restricted by law and is subject to all applicable federal, state, local and international laws and regulations.

04How to Enter

To enter and qualify, a participant must complete all of the following during the Promotion Period:

  • Follow French Atelier on Instagram and Meta (Facebook).
  • Share the official French Atelier channel link with friends, family and your network. A participant must make a minimum of ten (10) shares (channel-link messages sent) to qualify.
  • Capture a screenshot (print-screen) clearly showing at least ten (10) channel-link share messages you sent, as proof of entry.
  • Complete and submit the official entry form, attaching your screenshot.
A minimum of ten (10) qualifying shares is required to be eligible to win. Entries showing fewer than ten channel-link share messages will not be eligible. Limit one (1) entry per person; duplicate, automated, or fraudulent entries will be disqualified.

05Winner Selection & Odds

This is a skill- and merit-based contest, not a game of chance. From among all eligible entries, a French Atelier judging committee (the “Committee”) will select the three (3) participants whose sharing performance is judged strongest, applying the following criteria:

  • Shared activity — the breadth, consistency and authenticity of channel-link shares.
  • New channel followers reasonably attributable to the participant’s shares.
  • Engagement — the quality and genuineness of the reach generated.

A valid screenshot demonstrating the ten-share minimum is required for any entry to be considered. The Committee’s evaluations and decisions are final and binding in all respects. Because winners are selected on merit, the odds of winning depend on the number and quality of eligible entries received.

06Prize & Approximate Value

  • Three (3) prizes are available. Each winner receives one (1) round-trip economy-class flight between an eligible US airport and Paris, France, on dates within the eligible travel window (Section 7).
  • Each winner also receives access to the French Atelier three-day insider program of recommendations (neighborhoods, museums, cruises, terrasses and curated dining) as published on the Promotion landing page. This program consists of recommendations and itinerary guidance only.
  • Approximate retail value (“ARV”) of each prize: up to approximately US $1,500, depending on departure airport, carrier and booking dates. The ARV is a maximum; any difference between the stated ARV and the actual prize value will not be awarded.
  • The prize is awarded “as is,” is non-transferable, may not be substituted, and has no cash alternative, except that the Sponsor reserves the right, in its sole discretion, to substitute a prize (or component) of equal or greater value if the advertised prize becomes unavailable.
  • Not included: accommodation, ground transport, meals, beverages, gratuities, travel insurance, passport and visa fees, baggage charges, seat upgrades, in-flight purchases, and any other cost not expressly stated as included — all of which are the sole responsibility of the winner.

  • Before a prize is awarded, a potential winner may be required to verify eligibility and to execute and return an Affidavit of Eligibility, Liability Release and (where lawful) a Publicity Release, together with any travel details requested. Failure to comply within the time specified will result in forfeiture.
  • If a potential winner is found to be ineligible, cannot be contacted, declines the prize, or does not comply with these Official Rules, the prize may be forfeited and awarded to an alternate.
